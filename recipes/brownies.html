Brownies

Description

This recipe is for a delicious home made brownies, which are soft, chewy and chocolately.

This is a perfect recipe for a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 185g unsalted butter
  • 185g dark chocolate
  • 85g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate
  • 50g milk chocolate
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275g golden caster sugar

Steps

  1. Chop butter into small cubes and add to a bowl. Also add dark chocolate broken down into pieces
  2. Fill a saucepan with water and boil over a low heat. Sit the butter/chocolate bowl on top of the pan and keep over heat until fully melted.
  3. Remove bowl and leave to cool
  4. Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4.
  5. Sieve the plain flour and cocoa powder into a separate bowl and then chop the remaining chocolate into small chunks.
  6. Add eggs and caster sugar into another bowl and whisk until thick and creamy - check for a trail if unsure.
  7. Fold the melted chocolate into the egg mixture until completely mixed and just a dark brown colour. After this add the cocoa and flour mix and fold in until no longer dusty and looks sticky and fudgy. Then stir in chocolate chunks.
  8. Pour mixture into a greased tin and cover base completely, making sure mixture is levelled out evenly.
  9. Bake for 30 mins, add any extra time needed until its shiny, crisp and baked through.
  10. Leave to cool.
