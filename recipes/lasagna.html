Lasagna

Description

This recipe is for a delicious home cooked lasagna, which is saucy, meaty and cheesey.

This is a perfect dish for a meal with the family.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • 140g Pancetta
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 500g Pork Mince
  • 200ml Milk
  • 2 x 400g of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 500ml red wine
  • 400g dried pasta sheets
  • 50g Parmesan
  • 2 x Bechamel sauce
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. To make the meat sauce, put the oil, onion, carrot, garlic and pancetta in a large saucepan. On a medium high heat, cook until the veg is soft and golden.
  2. Add the beef and pork mince, the milk and chopped tomatoes and stir together making sure to break up any lumps of mince. After 5 - 10 minutes stir in the oregano, the stock cubes and the red wine, and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 45 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  3. Uncover and continue to simmer until the meat is tender and saucy.
  4. Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4. Layer the base of an oven proof dish with some of the meat sauce. Cover with a layer of dried pasta sheets, snapping them to fit if needed. Cover this completely with a quarter of the bechamel sauce. Then add a layer of the meat sauce and sprinkle over some of the parmesan.
  5. Repeat the above step twice more and add a final layer of pasta.
  6. Finally cover pasta sheets completely with the last of the bechamel and sprinkle on remaining parmesan. Bake for a further 45 minutes browned and crisp on top.
